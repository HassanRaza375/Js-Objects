Daily Timetable & Meal Plan
Time Meal/Activity Details
6:30 AM Wake & Hydrate Drink 300–400 ml water (add lemon for extra vitamin C).
7:00 AM Breakfast • 2–3 scrambled eggs (or egg whites with 1 whole egg) with spinach and tomatoes
• 1–2 slices whole-grain toast
• 1/2 avocado
• A serving of mixed berries or an apple
7:45 AM Light Stretch & Mobility 10-minute stretching or mobility routine.
10:00 AM Mid-Morning Snack • Greek yogurt (about 150g) with honey & a handful of nuts
• Or a small protein smoothie with unsweetened almond milk, protein powder, and fruit
12:30 PM Lunch • 100–120g lean protein (grilled chicken, turkey, or tofu)
• 1 cup quinoa or brown rice
• 1–2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers)
• Small salad with olive oil & lemon dressing
3:30 PM Pre-Workout Snack • A banana with a tablespoon of natural nut butter
• Or a small serving of oats with fruit
5:00 PM Gym/Workout Session Heavy lifting session; remember to stay hydrated during your workout.
6:30 PM Post-Workout Recovery • Protein shake (20–25g protein) mixed with water or milk
• A piece of fruit (e.g., half a banana or berries)
7:30 PM Dinner • 100–120g lean protein (fish, chicken, or plant-based option)
• 1 medium sweet potato or 1 cup whole-grain pasta
• Steamed or roasted vegetables (asparagus, zucchini, green beans)
• A drizzle of olive oil or a few slices of avocado
9:00 PM Optional Light Snack • Cottage cheese with a few berries
• Or a small handful of almonds with a piece of fruit
9:30–10:00 PM Evening Routine Wind down with a walk, stretching, or relaxation activities.
10:00 PM Bedtime Aim for 7–9 hours of quality sleep for optimal recovery.
